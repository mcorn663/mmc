ExploreIT: Knowledge Base & Training Hub

While working at Systems & Technology Research (STR), one project that I completed was the STR IT Knowledge Base & Training Hub. A goal for this project was to provide employees with a comprehensive digital resources to learn about the digital tools available at the company. At the end of this project, the IT Knowledge Base & Training hub contained over 300 articles and was visited over 400 times per week.

Key objectives:

  • Create articles on IT topics to the STR digital environment
  • Develop a hierarchical data structure
  • Use metadata to organize data
